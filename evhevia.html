Eduardo Valdes-Hévia


My name is Eduardo Valdés-Hevia and I am a digital artist from Spain, creating

strange worlds through photomanipulation.


I picked up my mother’s computer at age 13 and started learning Photoshop by

myself, and I have since worked in both the surreal and horror genres. In my

opinion, photomanipulation has the potential to show ideas in a completely

different light than traditional art, intertwining reality and narrative.


I have worked in advertising, RPGs, and created covers for books and music

albums. I have had the honour of being interviewed for CBC Nova Scotia and

Snopes and featured on adobe.com . My work has been published in the

Photographize and Chiiz magazines.


Other from art, I graduated from medical school in Oviedo, Spain.


If you want to know more, you can follow me on Twitter, Tumblr and Instagram

or watch me work live on Twitch. You may also join the Discord horror art server

I created, or support me directly on Patreon. For any business inquiries, feel free

to contact me at valdevia.art@gmail.com.
